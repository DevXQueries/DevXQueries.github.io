Quizzly

Exam preparation and progress tracking app for competitive exams.

Project Overview

The App is designed to help users prepare for competitive examinations (such as UPSC and similar exams) by providing practice quizzes, study materials, and comprehensive progress tracking. It uses a personalized approach to learning, allowing users to focus on their weak areas and improve efficiently.

Key Features

  • Practice quizzes and mock exams
  • Detailed progress tracking and analytics
  • Offline study material access
  • Secure Gmail authentication
  • Personalized recommendations
