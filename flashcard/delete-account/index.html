Account and Data Deletion Request

Users of the DevXQueries Flashcards app can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

🧾 How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to devxqueries@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: “Delete My DevXQueries Account”
  3. In the email body, include:
    • The email address used to sign in to the app
    • (Optional) Reason for deletion

Once the request is received, we will verify the account and process the deletion.

🗑️ What Data Is Deleted

When an account deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User authentication account (Firebase Authentication)
  • App usage data associated with the account

📦 Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

Some data may be retained for a limited period where required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Anonymous analytics data (non-identifiable)
  • Crash and performance logs (aggregated and anonymized)

Such data, if retained, is not linked to the user’s identity and is automatically deleted according to Google Firebase retention policies.

⏱️ Deletion Timeframe

  • Account and associated personal data are deleted within 30 days of receiving a valid request.
