Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DevXQueries (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Exam Preparation & Quiz App (“the App”).

1. Overview of the App

The App is designed to help users prepare for competitive examinations (such as UPSC and similar exams) by providing:

  • Practice quizzes
  • Study materials
  • Progress tracking

The App uses Google (Gmail) authentication for user login and tracks learning activity to improve the user experience.

2. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We collect limited personal information only for authentication purposes, including:

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication)

We do not collect phone numbers, physical addresses, or payment information.

b) Exam & Learning Activity Data

We collect app-specific activity data such as:

  • Number of exams or quizzes attempted
  • Quiz scores and progress
  • Study materials downloaded
  • Learning progress and completion status

This data is used only to track progress and personalize the learning experience.

c) Technical & Usage Data

We may collect non-personal technical information including:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash and performance logs

This data helps us improve app stability and performance.

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Track exam preparation progress
  • Improve quiz recommendations
  • Provide personalized learning insights
  • Improve app functionality and reliability

We do not sell or rent user data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

  • User progress and activity data may be stored securely using cloud services (such as Firebase).
  • Study materials downloaded by the user may be stored locally on the device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

🔹 Google / Firebase

  • Used for Gmail authentication
  • Used for analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information using industry-standard security practices. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal data, they may contact us to request deletion.

8. User Choices & Data Deletion

Users can:

  • View their learning progress within the app
  • Delete their account through the app or Firebase Authentication
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately.
